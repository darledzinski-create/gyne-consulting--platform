Referral & Escalation Notice

This online consultation service is intended for non-acute gynaecological concerns. Where a condition is identified that requires physical examination, investigations, or urgent intervention, appropriate referral will be advised.

Depending on clinical circumstances, patients may be advised to seek review by their General Practitioner, attend an urgent care facility, or present to emergency services.

If required, a GP liaison letter can be provided upon request.
