Communication Policy

Purpose of Communication

This online gynaecological consulting platform enables structured, professional communication between patients and Dr. Dariusz Ledzinski for the purpose of medical advice, guidance, and follow-up.

Communication Channels

Communication may take place through:

  • The secure consultation form on this platform
  • Email correspondence
  • WhatsApp messaging, when explicitly agreed

All communication is handled personally by Dr. Dariusz Ledzinski.

Response Times

Most messages are answered within 24 hours. Messages sent outside normal working hours may be answered the next working day. Weekends and public holidays may result in longer response times.

If no response is received within 48 hours, patients are welcome to follow up.

Use of WhatsApp

WhatsApp may be used as a supplementary communication tool when both parties agree. Its use is limited to clarification, follow-up communication, and administrative coordination where appropriate.

Scope of Communication

Communication through this platform is intended to support clinical assessment, explanation of findings, discussion of management options, and ongoing professional guidance.

Acknowledgement

By using this platform, you acknowledge and accept this Communication Policy.
