First Consultation Package

The First Consultation is designed to provide a safe, confidential, and professional initial medical assessment through an online format.

What the First Consultation Includes

  • Careful review of your medical history and current concerns
  • Assessment of symptoms based on the information you provide
  • Clear medical advice and guidance
  • Discussion of possible next steps
The consultation focuses on understanding your situation and advising you on the most appropriate course of action.

Possible Outcomes

Following the consultation, one or more of the following may occur:

  • Medical advice and reassurance
  • Recommendation for in-person examination
  • Referral to a GP or specialist
  • Issuing of a prescription or medical document, where appropriate

What the Consultation Does Not Include

The First Consultation does not replace a physical examination or emergency medical care.
  • No guarantee of prescriptions or certificates
  • No management of emergency or acute medical conditions
  • No ongoing follow-up unless agreed separately

Confidentiality

All information shared during your consultation is treated as strictly confidential and handled in accordance with professional standards.

Important Note

Online consultations have limitations. If at any point your condition requires urgent or in-person assessment, you will be advised accordingly.
