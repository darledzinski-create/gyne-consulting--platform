First Consultation Package

Your first consultation is designed to give you the time, privacy, and professional attention needed to understand your concern properly.

This is a medical consultation — not a rushed exchange of messages.

Step 1: Your Story

You are invited to describe your concern in your own words. There is no need to use medical terminology.

You may include:

  • when the problem started
  • how it has changed over time
  • what improves or worsens it
  • relevant medical history or medication

Step 2: Clarifying Questions

After reviewing your message, I may ask follow-up questions to clarify important details. This is a normal and essential part of good medical care.

Step 3: Medical Explanation

I will explain what may be happening medically, what conditions are being considered, and what can reasonably be assessed through telemedicine.

Step 4: Advice and Management

If appropriate, you may receive medical advice, reassurance, or recommendations for further assessment.

Important Limitations

Online consultations do not replace physical examination where required and are not suitable for acute or emergency conditions.

Personal Note
This service is built on careful listening, respect, and professional integrity. You will be treated as a person — not a checklist.
